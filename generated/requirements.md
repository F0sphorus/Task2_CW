Actors:
- Librarian
- Library Member
- Administrator

Functional Requirements:
- The system shall allow librarians to add, update, and remove books from the catalog.
- The system shall allow members to search, view, and filter books by title, author, category, and availability.
- The system shall allow members to borrow and return books.
- The system shall track due dates and calculate overdue fines.
- The system shall allow librarians to reserve books for members and manage holds.
- The system shall maintain member records, including registration and status.
- The system shall send notifications for due dates, overdue items, reservations, and account status changes.
- The system shall generate reports on circulation, inventory, overdue items, and member activity.
- The system shall support administrator functions for user role management and system configuration.

Non-functional Requirements:
- The system shall provide role-based access control.
- The system shall ensure data accuracy and consistency for books, loans, and member records.
- The system shall respond to search requests within acceptable performance limits.
- The system shall protect sensitive user data through secure authentication and authorization.
- The system shall maintain high availability for daily library operations.
- The system shall provide audit logs for important transactions and administrative actions.
- The system shall be scalable to support growth in books, members, and transactions.
- The system shall be usable by staff and members with minimal training.
